Biography Panel

The Origin Story

Before Empire Rocks, the jewellery world was in darkness. People were just... wearing things.

The Early Years
Born in South Africa with an eye for the extraordinary. While other children collected stickers, young Leah was reportedly critiquing the cut quality of her grandmother's engagement ring. She was four. Her assessment was correct.
The Awakening
A pivotal moment that gemologists now refer to as "The Awakening." Leah realized that the world did not need more mass produced jewellery. It needed pieces that made people feel something. Something profound. Something that made proposals go viral.
Empire Rocks is Founded
Based in Cape Town with a presence in Johannesburg, Empire Rocks launched as a bespoke fine jewellery house. The name is a masterclass in understatement. Leah didn't just enter the industry. The industry rearranged itself around her.
The Present Day
Now operating by appointment only across two cities, Leah curates and creates fine jewellery using only the finest materials. Custom engagement rings, chains, charms, earrings, and bands. Each piece is a small act of genius. The waitlist is, at this point, more of a lifestyle.
